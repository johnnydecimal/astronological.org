About us – what is astronology?

Astronology is a system of logic that assumes celestial objects have no influence on human affairs or terrestrial events.

We reject the tradition of assigning a star/zodiac sign to all humans at birth without their permission. We don’t think that it’s ‘just a bit of fun’. We deplore that astrology, a practice built on lies and quackery, is supported by mainstream media outlets.

Our goals include:

  • Standing up for logical thinking and evidence-based science.
  • Providing a forum for humans to publicly reject the pseudoscience of astrology.
  • Encouraging more humans to explore astrology’s lack of logic and evidence.
  • Highlighting the conflict of interest of those who peddle astrology myths for profit.
